About Hope and Adoption Shelter

Love heals

Set up over seven years ago, and with the sole purpose of rescuing the stray, abandoned animals of Nicaragua, Hope and Adoption Shelter currently cares for over 123 dogs and cats.


Based in Managua, in the west of Nicaragua, the rescued dogs and cats have up to 170 square meters of private, enclosed groves to run around in.


With very few people willing to help, there are still an overwhelming number of stray cats and dogs in the country that need rescuing. In the past, dogs have been left tied-up, often with their puppies in a box, outside of the shelter.

Over 70 of the shelter’s cats and dogs have been neutered with only a remaining few left, due to their age or health.


This shelter receives no help from the authorities and relies solely on donations.


Since 2014, Hope and Adoption Shelter has re-homed over 400 animals. Yet, there are hundreds of dogs and cats still looking for their Forever Homes, so please visit the Adoption page to find your best friend.
