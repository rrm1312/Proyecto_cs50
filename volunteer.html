We are looking for
committed people
We are looking for your commitment and your energy to help us in our work, you are an essential part of the functioning of our shelter.

Volunteering
We need people with a desire to help the animals, whether it be walking dogs, petting cats, cleaning the facilities, running washing machines, handing out food, etc. Volunteers are an essential part of running the shelter and require a long-term commitment to the animals. If you want to be a volunteer of the Lliga and you meet the necessary requirements, click on the form at the bottom of this page and fill it out.

Requeriments

  • Minimum of one year as a volunteer
  • Attendance at least every two weeks,in a schedule of
    4 hours each day.
  • Be punctual in the chosen shift
  • Be of legal age

    • Turns

    Modalidad de gatos: De Lunes a Domingo, de 9:30 a 13:30h
    Modalidad de perros pequeños: De Lunes a Viernes, de 9 a 13h o de 15 a 18hSábados y Domingos, de 8 a 12h o de 13:30 a 16h
    Modalidad de perros grandes: De Lunes a Viernes, de 8 a 12h o de 14:30 a 18hSábados y Domingos, de 8 a 12h o de 13 a 16h

    Being a volunteer entails a high level of emotional sensitivity, responsibility and long-term commitment. The role of the volunteer is fundamental to help in the care of the shelter animals and to make their stay more enjoyable and happy. In order to cover all shifts and the needs of the shelter animals, volunteer tasks must be performed on schedule and attended with maximum punctuality.

    Before filling out the form you should make sure that you meet the above requirements, that you have enough time to make a long-term commitment and that it fits your idea of volunteering. To do so, please read our Volunteering Plan and our Guidelines before you sign up. If you don't think you can make that commitment, you can opt for sponsorship.
